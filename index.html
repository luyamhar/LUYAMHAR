Our Story

A pair of passionate individuals bounded by their goal to create fun & exciting, stress & worry free, memorable & amazing events for every couple, celebrants and families. Preppy Pair PH has been in the events scene since 2014, servicing couples and families making their dream events a reality. Ensuring impeccable execution of events with class & style.

Say hello to Pepper. She has been in the Corporate Events and Advertising industry for the past 8 years, with an exceptional roster of Clients and Projects. Her expertise in project management, marketing, business planning and creative strategy, not to mention her meticulous nature, definitely makes her ahead of her game. She’s been making dream weddings and parties possible together with the support and love of her Preppy Partner, Jemma.

Jemma, also a Preppy Pair bride, started to collaborate with Pepper when they planned her own wedding. Jemma’s natural love and passion for DIY, skills in creating one of a kind crafts and unending search for uniqueness and creativity paved way in coming up with new features and offers of Preppy Pair PH.

Come and journey with us as we extend our utmost love and care, preparing your dream events just like our own.
